Why is conservation of critical minerals so important?

Critical minerals are essential in many global industries and inventions, such as computers. They are also utilized in clean energy technologies to counteract fossil-fuel based counterparts. The limited quantities of these minerals and their integrity to modern life mean that they should be conserved through recycling processes so they can be re-used!

Why recycling centers?

Recycling centers will make the process of redirecting used minerals to be re-utilized a viable option. This will help bridge the gap between supply and demand of critical minerals that has been expanding due to the growing pre-eminence of industries that rely on such minerals (ex. electric vehicles, computer hardware).
